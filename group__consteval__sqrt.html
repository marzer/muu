Math » consteval_sqrt() module

std::sqrt alternatives that are always available at compile time.

Contents

  • Reference
    • Functions

Warning

These functions are implemented such that they are always available at compile time, arriving at their results using very slow iterative machinery. Do not use them at runtime!
