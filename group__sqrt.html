Math » sqrt() module

Constexpr-friendly alternatives to std::sqrt.

Contents

  • Reference
    • Functions

Conditional availability

Older compilers won't provide the necessary machinery for sqrt to work in constexpr contexts. You can check for constexpr support by examining build::supports_constexpr_math.
