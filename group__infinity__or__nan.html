Math » infinity_or_nan() module

Checks for infinities and not-a-numbers (NaN).

Contents

  • Reference
    • Functions

Conditional availability

Older compilers won't provide the necessary machinery for infinity and NaN checks to work in constexpr contexts. You can check for constexpr support by examining build::supports_constexpr_infinity_or_nan.
